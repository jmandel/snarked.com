# Banana Bread (Refined)
Whole wheat banana bread with less sugar, olive oil, and optional carrots

## Quick facts
- Active: ~1 h 15 min
- Heat: 350 degrees F
- Stations: oven

## Process layout
- Step: cream-fats-sugar
- Step: add-wet
- Step: combine-dry
- Step: make-batter
- Step: bake

## Steps
### 1. Cream Fats Sugar
Timing: 0 min
Cream butter + oil and sugar.
Uses:
  - 4 Tbsp butter (56 g)
  - 4 Tbsp olive oil (56 g)
  - 1 c sugar (200 g)
Makes: creamed fats and sugar

### 2. Add Wet
Timing: 0 min
Add eggs, then bananas and/or carrots. Stir in yogurt and vanilla.
Uses:
  - creamed fats and sugar
  - 2 eggs
  - fruit or vegetable choice: banana option: 2-3 bananas OR banana-carrot option: 1 banana + 2 carrots — mashed or grated; choose one source option
  - 1/4 c low-fat plain yogurt (61 g)
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract (4.2 g)
Makes: banana wet mix

### 3. Combine Dry
Timing: 0 min
In another bowl, soft together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and chips/nuts.
Uses:
  - 1 1/2 c whole wheat flour (180 g)
  - 1 tsp baking soda (4.6 g)
  - 1/4 tsp baking powder (1 g)
  - 1/4 tsp salt (1.5 g)
  - 1/2 c chocolate chips and/or nuts (85 g)
Makes: dry mix

### 4. Make Batter
Timing: 0 min
Mix dry ingredients with wet until just combined.
Uses:
  - banana wet mix
  - dry mix
Makes: refined banana bread batter
Notes:
- Source timing: until just combined

### 5. Bake
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
Pour into a greased loaf pan and cook for 75 minutes at 350 degrees F.
Uses:
  - refined banana bread batter
Makes: refined banana bread
Notes:
- Heat: 350 degrees F
- Source timing: 75 minutes

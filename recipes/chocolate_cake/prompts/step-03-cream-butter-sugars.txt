# Chocolate cake

## Steps
### 1. Sift Dry
Timing: 0 min
Into a small bowl sift together flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, and salt.
Uses:
  - 2 1/4 cups cake flour (260 g) — Not self-rising.
  - 3/4 cup unsweetened Dutch-process cocoa powder (64 g)
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 g)
Makes: flour cocoa mix

### 2. Mix Sour Cream
Timing: 0 min
In another small bowl stir together baking soda and sour cream.
Uses:
  - 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (1.2 g)
  - 1 cup / 8 1/2 ounces sour cream (240 g)
Makes: sour cream mixture

### 3. Cream Butter Sugars
Timing: 0 min
Beat together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
Uses:
  - 2 1/4 sticks / 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (740 g) — softened
  - 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar (300 g)
  - 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar (330 g) — packed
Makes: light fluffy butter sugar base
Notes:
- Source timing: about 10 minutes
- Method: electric mixer

### 4. Add Extracts Eggs
Timing: 10 min
Beat in extracts and add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition.
Uses:
  - light fluffy butter sugar base
  - 1 tablespoon chocolate extract — optional
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (4.2 g)
  - 6 large eggs
Makes: egg extract base
Notes:
- Method: electric mixer

### 5. Make Batter
Timing: 10 min
With mixer at low speed, add flour mixture and sour cream mixture alternately in batches, beating until just combined.
Uses:
  - egg extract base
  - flour cocoa mix
  - sour cream mixture
Makes: chocolate cake batter
Notes:
- Source timing: until just combined
- Method: mixer at low speed

### 6. Bake Cake
Timing: 10 min
Station/tools: oven
Butter and flour a 12-cup bundt pan. Pour batter into the pan and put in middle of cold oven. Set oven to 350 F and bake cake 1 hour and 25 minutes, or until a tester comes out clean.
Uses:
  - chocolate cake batter
Makes: baked chocolate cake
Notes:
- Heat: cold oven set to 350 F
- Source timing: 1 hour 25 minutes, or until a tester comes out clean

### 7. Cool Cake
Timing: 95 min
Cool cake in pan on a rack 15 minutes and turn out onto rack to cool completely.
Uses:
  - baked chocolate cake
Makes: cooled chocolate cake
Notes:
- Source timing: 15 minutes in pan, then until completely cool

### 8. Make Glaze
Timing: 110 min
Station/tools: stovetop
Chop bittersweet chocolate and melt it with butter in a double boiler or metal bowl set over barely simmering water, stirring until smooth.
Uses:
  - 3 ounces fine-quality bittersweet chocolate (85 g) — chopped; Not unsweetened.
  - 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (36 g)
Makes: smooth chocolate glaze
Notes:
- Source timing: until smooth
- Method: double boiler or metal bowl over barely simmering water

### 9. Pipe Glaze
Timing: 110 min
Transfer glaze to a pastry bag or plastic bag and pipe glaze back and forth over top of cake, letting it drip down sides.
Uses:
  - cooled chocolate cake
  - smooth chocolate glaze
Makes: glazed chocolate cake
Notes:
- Method: pastry bag #3 tip or snipped plastic bag

### 10. Set Glaze
Timing: 110 min
Station/tools: counter
Let glaze set 30 minutes at room temperature.
Uses:
  - glazed chocolate cake
Makes: set glazed cake
Notes:
- Source timing: 30 minutes

### 11. Serve
Timing: 140 min
Serve cake with dollops of sour cream and strawberries.
Uses:
  - set glazed cake
  - sour cream and strawberries — optional
Makes: served chocolate cake

# Za'atar Salmon &amp; Tahini
Salmon baked over wilted greens with za'atar, sumac, lemon, and tahini

## Quick facts
- Serves: 4
- Prep: ~5 min
- Cook: ~20 min
- Heat: 450 F
- Stations: stovetop + oven

## Process layout
- Step: preheat-oven
- Step: season-salmon
- Step: wilt-greens
- Covered-time group: parallel
  - Why: While the pan bakes, whisk the tahini sauce.
  - Oven: first-bake
  - Counter: whisk-tahini-sauce
  - Converge: Tahini sauce joins the pan for the finish bake.
- Step: finish-bake
- Step: garnish-and-serve

## Steps
### 1. Preheat Oven
Timing: 0 min · 1 minute · wait heats while you prep
Station/tools: oven
Preheat the oven to 450 F.
Notes:
- Let the oven heat while you season the fish and wilt the greens.

### 2. Season Salmon
Timing: 0 min · 3 minutes
Station/tools: counter, small bowl
Pat salmon dry, season with salt and pepper, then cover the tops with mixed za'atar and sumac.
Uses:
  - 4 fillets (1 lb 5 oz) salmon fillets (600 g) — skin on, pin bones removed
  - 2 T za'atar (12 g)
  - 2 t sumac (4 g)
  - to taste kosher salt and black pepper
Makes: za'atar-crusted salmon

### 3. Wilt Greens
Timing: 3 min · 2-3 minutes
Station/tools: stovetop, oven-safe saute pan
Heat oil in a large oven-safe saute pan, then cook greens with salt and pepper until wilted.
Uses:
  - 1 T olive oil (14 g)
  - 9 oz baby spinach or tender greens (250 g)
  - pinch kosher salt and black pepper
Makes: wilted greens
Notes:
- Use the pan that will go into the oven.

### 4. First Bake
Timing: 6 min · 1 minute · wait 5 minutes
Station/tools: oven, oven-safe saute pan
Set salmon skin-side down on the greens, drizzle with oil, and bake 5 minutes.
Uses:
  - wilted greens
  - za'atar-crusted salmon
  - 2 T olive oil (27 g)
Makes: salmon over wilted greens
Notes:
- This brief bake creates the covered time for whisking the sauce.

### 5. Whisk Tahini Sauce
Timing: 7 min · 4 minutes
Station/tools: small bowl, whisk
Whisk tahini, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and water until smooth and runny.
Uses:
  - 1/3 c tahini (90 g)
  - 3 cloves garlic (3 cloves) — minced
  - 2 1/2 T lemon juice (38 g)
  - 7 T water (100 g)
  - pinch kosher salt
Makes: runny tahini sauce
Notes:
- Do this while the first bake is in the oven.

### 6. Finish Bake
Timing: 12 min · 1 minute · wait 5 minutes
Station/tools: oven, oven-safe saute pan
Pour tahini around the salmon, not on top, and bake until the fish is cooked through and the tahini bubbles.
Uses:
  - salmon over wilted greens
  - runny tahini sauce
Makes: baked salmon with bubbling tahini
Notes:
- Keep the tahini around the fish so the spice crust stays visible.

### 7. Garnish and Serve
Timing: 18 min · 2 minutes
Station/tools: counter
Spoon lemon juice and olive oil over the pan, then finish with cilantro and extra sumac.
Uses:
  - baked salmon with bubbling tahini
  - 1 T lemon juice (15 g)
  - 1 T olive oil (14 g)
  - 1 1/2 T cilantro leaves (4 g) — roughly chopped
  - 1/2 t sumac (1 g)
Makes: za'atar salmon and tahini greens
Notes:
- Serve shortly after cooking; cooked tahini is best fresh.

# Over-Size Chocolate Chip Cookies
Thick and chewy over-size chocolate chip cookies from Cook's Illustrated

## Quick facts
- Active: ~18 min
- Heat: 325 F
- Stations: oven

## Process layout
- Step: whisk-dry
- Step: mix-wet
- Step: make-dough
- Step: form-cookies
- Step: bake
- Step: cool

## Steps
### 1. Whisk Dry
Timing: 0 min
Whisk flower, soda, salt in medium bowl.
Uses:
  - 2 c + 2 T flour (255 g) — Source says 'flower' in method.
  - 1/2 t baking soda (2.3 g)
  - 1/2 t salt (3 g)
Makes: dry mix

### 2. Mix Wet
Timing: 0 min
Mix butter and sugars until combined. Add egg, yolk, and vanilla.
Uses:
  - 12 T butter (170 g) — melted and cooled
  - 1 c dark brown sugar (220 g) — packed
  - 1/2 c granulated sugar (100 g)
  - 1 large egg
  - 1 egg yolk
  - 2 t vanilla extract (8.4 g)
Makes: butter and sugar mix

### 3. Make Dough
Timing: 0 min
Add dry ingredients and beat until combined. Add chips.
Uses:
  - butter and sugar mix
  - dry mix
  - 3/2 c chocolate chips (255 g)
Makes: cookie dough

### 4. Form Cookies
Timing: 0 min
Form cookies, about 1/4 c each, and tear to create an attractive finish.
Uses:
  - cookie dough
Makes: formed torn cookies

### 5. Bake
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
Heat oven to 325, grease baking sheets, and bake until light golden brown with soft puffy centers.
Uses:
  - formed torn cookies
Makes: baked cookies
Notes:
- Heat: 325 F
- Source timing: 15 to 18 minutes

### 6. Cool
Timing: 18 min
Cool on sheets.
Uses:
  - baked cookies
Makes: cooled chocolate chip cookies

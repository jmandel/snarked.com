# Black Forest Fudge
Black, foresty, fudgy

## Quick facts
- Active: ~20 min
- Heat: medium heat / 230 degrees F, medium high heat
- Stations: stovetop + refrigerator

## Process layout
- Step: make-chocolate-base
- Step: dissolve-syrup
- Step: boil-syrup
- Step: melt-chocolate
- Step: pan-fudge
- Step: chill

## Steps
### 1. Make Chocolate Base
Timing: 0 min
Put the first four ingredients in a metal bowl.
Uses:
  - 6 ounces bittersweet chocolate (170 g) — chopped
  - .5 cup marshmallow fluff
  - 1 ounce unsweetened chocolate (28 g) — chopped
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla (4.2 g)
Makes: chocolate base

### 2. Dissolve Syrup
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: stovetop
Mix the sugar, condensed milk, cherry preserves, whipping cream, water, and butter in a heavy saucepan. Stir over medium heat until butter melts and sugar dissolves.
Uses:
  - 1.25 cups sugar (250 g)
  - 0.5 cup condensed milk (120 g)
  - 0.5 cup cherry preserves
  - 0.5 cup whipping cream (120 g)
  - 1/3 cup water (80 g)
  - 0.25 cup butter (57 g)
Makes: dissolved sugar syrup
Notes:
- Heat: medium heat

### 3. Boil Syrup
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: stovetop
Add the dried cherries. Attach a candy thermometer, increase the heat to medium high, and boil until the mixture reaches 230 degrees F, stirring constantly but slowly.
Uses:
  - dissolved sugar syrup
  - 2/3 cup dried tart cherries
Makes: hot cherry syrup
Notes:
- Heat: 230 degrees F, medium high heat
- Source timing: about 17 minutes
- Method: candy thermometer

### 4. Melt Chocolate
Timing: 17 min
Pour the hot syrup immediately over the chocolate mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon until the chocolate melts and the mixture thickens a little.
Uses:
  - hot cherry syrup
  - chocolate base
Makes: fudge mixture
Notes:
- Source timing: about 3 minutes
- Method: wooden spoon

### 5. Pan Fudge
Timing: 20 min
Line a loaf pan with foil, pour the fudge mixture into the prepared pan, and smooth the top.
Uses:
  - fudge mixture
Makes: smoothed fudge in pan

### 6. Chill
Timing: 20 min
Station/tools: refrigerator
Chill until firm, about half an hour.
Uses:
  - smoothed fudge in pan
Makes: black forest fudge
Notes:
- Source timing: about half an hour

## Ingredients

Dry:

- 1.25 C Yellow cornmeal
- .75 C flour
- 1.75 tsp baking powder
- .75 tsp salt

Wet:

- 1.67 C milk
- 4 Tbsp melted butter
- .25 C Maple syrup
- 2 eggs

## Instructions

Mix the dry and the wet separately, then combine without overmixing to form a thin batter.  Serves 3---4.

## Ingredients

- large yellow onion, peeled and grated
- 1 lb ground meat (lamb, beef, chicken thighs, whatever)
- 1 clove garlic peeled and finely chopped
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1 pinch saffron threads
- 1 tbsp butter, melted
- ground sumac & lemon wedges (optional garnish)

## Instructions

Squeeze grated onion through cheesecloth to drain.  Mix onion, spices, and meat, and form into 8 logs.  Crumble  saffron into melted butter;  use this mixture to baste kebabs while grilling.

Recipe adapted from <http://www.paisleysky.net/foodnerd/archives/000411.html>

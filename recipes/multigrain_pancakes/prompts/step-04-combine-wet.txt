# Cooks' Multigrain Pancakes
Multigrain pancakes worth eating

## Quick facts
- Active: ~2 min
- Stations: stovetop

## Process layout
- Step: thicken-milk
- Step: process-muesli
- Step: combine-dry
- Step: combine-wet
- Step: make-batter
- Step: cook

## Steps
### 1. Thicken Milk
Timing: 0 min
Whisk lemon juice and milk in bowl; set aside to thicken.
Uses:
  - 4 t lemon juice (20 g)
  - 2 c whole milk (480 g)
Makes: thickened lemon milk
Notes:
- Source timing: set aside to thicken

### 2. Process Muesli
Timing: 0 min
Process 1.25 c muesli in food processor 2 minutes, transfer to large bowl.
Uses:
  - 1.25 c no-sugar-added muesli (250 g)
Makes: processed muesli
Notes:
- Source timing: 2 minutes
- Method: food processor

### 3. Combine Dry
Timing: 2 min
Add remaining unground muesli, flours, brown sugar, baking powder and soda, and salt. Whisk to combine.
Uses:
  - processed muesli
  - 3 T no-sugar-added muesli (38 g) — unground
  - .75 c unbleached white flour (90 g)
  - .5 c whole wheat flower — Source says 'flower'; preserved as written in label.
  - 2 T light or dark brown sugar (25 g)
  - 2.25 t baking powder (9 g)
  - .5 t baking soda (2.3 g)
  - .5 t table salt (3 g)
Makes: multigrain dry mix

### 4. Combine Wet
Timing: 2 min
Whisk eggs, melted butter, and vanilla into milk until combined.
Uses:
  - thickened lemon milk
  - 2 large eggs
  - 3 T unsalted butter (54 g) — melted and cooled
  - .75 t vanilla (3.2 g)
Makes: egg milk mixture

### 5. Make Batter
Timing: 2 min
Mix well in center of dry ingredients in boowl, pour in milk mixture and whisk very gently until just combined.
Uses:
  - multigrain dry mix
  - egg milk mixture
Makes: multigrain batter
Notes:
- Source timing: until just combined

### 6. Cook
Timing: 2 min
Station/tools: stovetop
Cook.
Uses:
  - multigrain batter
  - as needed vegetable oil
Makes: multigrain pancakes

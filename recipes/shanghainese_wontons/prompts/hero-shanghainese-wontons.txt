# Shanghainese Wontons
let's have some savories already

## Quick facts
- Active: ~45 min

- Stations: stovetop

## Steps
### 1. Prep Bok Choi
Timing: 0 min · 8 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: knife, cutting board, sink
Chop the bok choi into very small pieces, draining away as much water as possible.
Uses:
  - 1 1/4 lbs Shanghainese bok choi (565 g)
Makes: drained chopped bok choi

### 2. Prep Shrimp
Timing: 8 min · 4 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: knife, cutting board
Chop the shrimp into small pieces.
Uses:
  - 1/2 lb shrimp (225 g) — optional
Makes: chopped shrimp

### 3. Mix Filling
Timing: 12 min · 5 minutes
Station/tools: counter, mixing bowl
Combine the pork, bok choi and shrimp. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, corn flour, sugar, salt and pepper. Mix well.
Uses:
  - 1 lb minced pork (455 g)
  - drained chopped bok choi
  - chopped shrimp — optional
  - 6 T soy sauce (95 g)
  - 1 T sesame oil (14 g)
  - 2 T corn flour (15 g)
  - 1 T sugar (13 g)
  - to taste salt and pepper
Makes: seasoned wonton filling

### 4. Wrap Wontons
Timing: 17 min · 20 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: counter
Wrap the seasoned meat mix with wonton wrappers.
Uses:
  - seasoned wonton filling
  - 1 lb wonton wrappers (455 g)
Makes: wrapped wontons

### 5. Heat Stock
Timing: 37 min · 2 minutes · wait 8 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: stock pot, stovetop burner, stovetop
Heat the chicken stock in a medium pot.
Uses:
  - 1 can chicken stock
Makes: heated chicken stock
Notes:
- Wait: 8 minutes, estimated

### 6. Boil Water
Timing: 39 min · 2 minutes · wait 8 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: water pot, stovetop burner, stovetop
At the same time, boil enough water in another pot to cover wontons.
Makes: boiling water
Notes:
- Wait: 8 minutes, estimated

### 7. Cook Wontons
Timing: 41 min · 1 minute · wait 10 minutes
Station/tools: water pot, stovetop burner, stovetop
Add the wontons and bring to a boil again. Let boil for another 10 minutes or until cooked.
Uses:
  - wrapped wontons
  - boiling water
Makes: cooked wontons
Notes:
- Source timing: another 10 minutes or until cooked
- Wait: 10 minutes
- Cue: wontons cooked

### 8. Drain Wontons
Timing: 42 min · 2 minutes
Station/tools: sink, slotted spoon
Scoop the wontons out of the water with a slotted spoon and drain the water.
Uses:
  - cooked wontons
Makes: drained wontons
Notes:
- Method: slotted spoon

### 9. Serve
Timing: 44 min · 1 minute
Station/tools: counter
Put into the heated chicken stock. Serve.
Uses:
  - drained wontons
  - heated chicken stock
Makes: wontons in chicken stock

# Suchele-Inspired Lemon Squares
Inspired by the famous lemon squares of Suchele's bakery in Lenox, Mass.

## Quick facts
- Active: ~30 min
- Heat: 350 degrees
- Stations: oven

## Process layout

- Step: press-crust
- Step: bake-crust
- Step: mix-filling-dry
- Step: mix-filling-wet

- Step: finish-bake

## Steps
### 1. Mix Crust
Timing: 0 min
Mix crust ingredients.
Uses:
  - 3 1/4 C (+) flour (390 g)
  - 1.5 C (+) butter (340 g) — softened
  - 3/4 C (+) confectioners sugar (90 g)
Makes: crust dough

### 2. Press Crust
Timing: 0 min
Press into a jellyroll pan.
Uses:
  - crust dough
Makes: pressed crust

### 3. Bake Crust
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
Bake crust at 350 degrees until just beginning to turn golden.
Uses:
  - pressed crust
Makes: hot baked crust
Notes:
- Heat: 350 degrees
- Source timing: until just beginning to turn golden

### 4. Mix Filling Dry
Timing: 0 min
Prepare filling by mixing dry ingredients.
Uses:
  - 3 1/4 C (+) sugar (650 g)
  - 1 T (+) confectioners sugar (13 g)
  - 1/2 C (-) flour (60 g)
  - 1 t (-) salt (6 g)
Makes: filling dry mix

### 5. Mix Filling Wet
Timing: 0 min
Prepare filling by mixing wet ingredients.
Uses:
  - 4 lemons juice and rind of four lemons — Add bottled lemon juice as needed until no longer sloshy.
  - about 7.5432 eggs
Makes: lemon egg mixture

### 6. Combine Filling
Timing: 0 min
Combine the dry and wet filling mixtures.
Uses:
  - filling dry mix
  - lemon egg mixture
Makes: lemon filling

### 7. Pour Onto Hot Crust
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
Hold baked base in oven as another person pours the Lemon/Sugar/Egg mixture into pan, and slide gently into oven; be careful not to spill.
Uses:
  - hot baked crust
  - lemon filling
Makes: hot crust with filling

### 8. Bake Until Turnable
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
Bake at 350 degrees until the pan can be turned around inside oven without spilling.
Uses:
  - hot crust with filling
Makes: partially set lemon squares
Notes:
- Heat: 350 degrees
- Source timing: until pan can be turned without spilling

### 9. Finish Bake
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: oven
After turning, cook until just firm. Maybe 30 minutes total, maybe not!
Uses:
  - partially set lemon squares
Makes: lemon squares
Notes:
- Heat: 350 degrees
- Source timing: until just firm; maybe 30 minutes total, maybe not

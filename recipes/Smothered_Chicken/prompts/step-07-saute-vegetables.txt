# Smothered Chicken + Barley
Baked chicken with barley, tomatoes, and lots of spices

## Quick facts
- Active: ~40 min
- Wait: ~1 h 15 min
- Heat: medium high heat / 350 F
- Stations: stovetop + oven

## Steps
### 1. Make Spice Mixture
Timing: 0 min · 2 minutes
Station/tools: counter, small bowl
Combine the first six ingredients in a small bowl.
Uses:
  - 1 t ground cumin (2.1 g)
  - 1/2 t chili powder (1.4 g)
  - 1/2 t salt (3 g)
  - 1/2 t ground cinnamon (1.3 g)
  - 1/8 t garlic powder (0.4 g)
  - 1/8 t pepper (0.3 g)
Makes: spice mixture

### 2. Chop Onions
Timing: 2 min · 5 minutes
Station/tools: knife, cutting board
Chop the onions for the barley mixture.
Uses:
  - 1 1/2 c onions (240 g)
Makes: chopped onions

### 3. Chop Red Pepper
Timing: 7 min · 3 minutes
Station/tools: knife, cutting board
Chop the red pepper for the barley mixture.
Uses:
  - 1 c red pepper (150 g)
Makes: chopped red pepper

### 4. Prep Tomatoes
Timing: 10 min · 3 minutes
Station/tools: sink, knife, cutting board
Drain and chop the tomatoes.
Uses:
  - 14.5 oz can tomatoes (410 g can)
Makes: drained chopped tomatoes

### 5. Rub Chicken
Timing: 13 min · 2 minutes
Station/tools: counter
Rub the chicken with half the resulting spice mixture.
Uses:
  - 6 chicken thighs
  - half spice mixture
Makes: spice-rubbed chicken

### 6. Brown Chicken
Timing: 15 min · 8 minutes, estimated
Station/tools: stovetop, skillet
Brown chicken in 2 T oil in skillet over medium high heat. Remove as it browns to baking pan.
Uses:
  - spice-rubbed chicken
  - 2 T olive oil (28 g)
Makes: browned chicken
Notes:
- Heat: medium high heat
- Cue: chicken browned and moved to baking pan

### 7. Saute Vegetables
Timing: 23 min · 4 minutes
Station/tools: stovetop, skillet
Add remaining oil to skillet. Saute onion and pepper a few minutes.
Uses:
  - remaining 2 T olive oil (remaining 28 g)
  - chopped onions
  - chopped red pepper
Makes: sauteed onion and pepper
Notes:
- Source timing: a few minutes

### 8. Build Barley Sauce
Timing: 27 min · 5 minutes
Station/tools: stovetop, skillet
Add soy sauce and continue cooking a few more minutes. Add broth, barley, tomatoes, and remaining spice mixture. Bring to boil.
Uses:
  - sauteed onion and pepper
  - 1 T soy sauce (16 g)
  - 3 1/2 c chicken broth (840 g)
  - 1 1/4 c uncooked barley (250 g)
  - drained chopped tomatoes
  - remaining half spice mixture
Makes: boiling barley mixture
Notes:
- Source timing: a few more minutes, then bring to boil
- Cue: mixture comes to a boil

### 9. Assemble
Timing: 32 min · 2 minutes
Station/tools: counter
Pour mixture over chicken in baking pan.
Uses:
  - boiling barley mixture
  - browned chicken
Makes: chicken covered with barley mixture

### 10. Bake
Timing: 34 min · 2 minutes · wait 1 hour
Station/tools: oven
Cover and bake 1 hour at 350 F.
Uses:
  - chicken covered with barley mixture
Makes: baked chicken and barley
Notes:
- Heat: 350 F
- Source timing: 1 hour
- Wait: 1 hour
- Cue: barley tender and chicken cooked

### 11. Slice Green Onions
Timing: 36 min · 3 minutes
Station/tools: knife, cutting board
Slice the green onions for garnish.
Uses:
  - 1 bunch green onions
Makes: sliced green onions

### 12. Rest And Garnish
Timing: 39 min · 1 minute · wait 15 minutes
Station/tools: counter
Let stand 15 minutes before serving. Top with sliced green onions.
Uses:
  - baked chicken and barley
  - sliced green onions
Makes: smothered chicken and barley
Notes:
- Source timing: 15 minutes
- Wait: 15 minutes

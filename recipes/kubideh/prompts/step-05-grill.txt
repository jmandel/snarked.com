# Kubideh
Kubideh with the meat of your choice

## Quick facts
- Stations: grill

## Steps
### 1. Drain Onion
Timing: 0 min
Squeeze grated onion through cheesecloth to drain.
Uses:
  - large yellow onion — peeled and grated
Makes: drained grated onion
Notes:
- Method: cheesecloth

### 2. Mix Meat
Timing: 0 min
Mix onion, spices, and meat.
Uses:
  - drained grated onion
  - 1 lb ground meat (455 g) — Lamb, beef, chicken thighs, or another chosen meat.
  - 1 clove garlic — peeled and finely chopped
  - 1 egg — lightly beaten
  - 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs (27 g)
  - 2 tsp salt (12 g)
  - 1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper (1.2 g)
  - 1/2 tsp ground turmeric (1.5 g)
  - 1/4 tsp paprika (0.6 g)
Makes: kubideh meat mixture

### 3. Form Logs
Timing: 0 min
Form into 8 logs.
Uses:
  - kubideh meat mixture
Makes: 8 formed kubideh logs

### 4. Make Baste
Timing: 0 min
Crumble saffron into melted butter.
Uses:
  - 1 pinch saffron threads
  - 1 tbsp butter (14 g) — melted
Makes: saffron butter

### 5. Grill
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: grill
Use this mixture to baste kebabs while grilling.
Uses:
  - 8 formed kubideh logs
  - saffron butter
Makes: grilled kebabs

### 6. Garnish
Timing: 0 min
Serve with ground sumac and lemon wedges if desired.
Uses:
  - grilled kebabs
  - ground sumac and lemon wedges — optional
Makes: kubideh

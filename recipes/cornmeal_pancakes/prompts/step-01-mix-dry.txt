# Cornmeal Pancakes

## Steps
### 1. Mix Dry
Timing: 0 min
Mix the dry ingredients separately.
Uses:
  - 1.25 C yellow cornmeal (200 g)
  - .75 C flour (90 g)
  - 1.75 tsp baking powder (7 g)
  - .75 tsp salt (4.5 g)
Makes: dry mix

### 2. Mix Wet
Timing: 0 min
Mix the wet ingredients separately.
Uses:
  - 1.67 C milk (400 g)
  - 4 Tbsp butter (56 g) — melted
  - .25 C maple syrup (81 g)
  - 2 eggs
Makes: wet mix

### 3. Make Batter
Timing: 0 min
Combine without overmixing to form a thin batter.
Uses:
  - dry mix
  - wet mix
Makes: thin batter

### 4. Cook
Timing: 0 min
Station/tools: stovetop
Cook as pancakes.
Uses:
  - thin batter
Makes: cornmeal pancakes

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c sugar
- 1/2 c butter
- 2 eggs
- 2-3 bananas, mashed
- 1/4 c french vanilla yogurt
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 c flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 c chocolate chips and/or nuts

## Instructions

Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs, then bananas, one at a time. Stir in yogurt and vanilla.

In another bowl, soft together flour, bakind soda, baking powder, salt, and chips/nuts. Mix dry ingredients with wet until just combined.

Pour into a greased loaf pan and cook for 75 minutes at 350 degrees F.

Cooks' Multigrain Pancakes
Submitted by: carrien
Date: 2007-10-28

Ingredients:
4 t lemon juice
2 c whole milk
1.25 c + 3 T no-sugar-added muesli
.75 c unbleached white flour
.5 c whole wheat flower
2 T light or dark brown sugar
2.25 t baking powder
.5 t baking soda
.5 t table salt
2 large eggs
3 T unsalted butter, melted and cooled
.75 t vanilla
vegetable oil


Instructions
  1. Whisk lemon juice and milk in bowl; set aside to thicken.
  2. Process 1.25 c muesli in food processor 2 minutes, transfer to large bowl. Add remaining unground muesli, flours, brown sugar, baking powder and soda, and salt. Whisk to combine.
  3. Whisk eggs, melted butter, and vanilla into milk until combined. Mix well in center of dry ingredients in boowl, pour in milk mixture and whisk very gently until just combined.
  4. Cook.
